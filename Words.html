The Words I Wish I Could Say to You

A Collection of My Deepest Feelings for You


I’ve been feeling lost for a long time. I thought I wouldn’t be able to receive true, unconditional love for the rest of my life. But I found you. I always remember the first day I met you. I remember everything in detail,your smell, what you wore, how you smiled at me. I love you with my whole heart. I love everything about you. If I have to say it in detail, it will be your pure heart. Whenever you speak to me, I melt. I love you today more than yesterday, and I will love you even more tomorrow. I truly appreciate your kindness, your effort, your love, your compassion toward me, and everything you have done for me. I want to keep this memory forever. I promise you that I will love and support you forever. I will be by your side in your ups and downs. You belong to me. I love you, my sweetest boy.
